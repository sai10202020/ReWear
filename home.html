About Us

ReWear is an initiative dedicated to promoting sustainable fashion by encouraging donations of gently-used clothing. Our mission is to reduce textile waste and support underprivileged communities by giving clothes a second life. Every item donated through our platform goes to people who truly need them, creating a ripple of kindness and sustainability.
